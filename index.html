What the Demo Is

A phone-in voice demo that allows your team to call and ask questions about Radiology Imaging Associates and Radiology Regional — two RadNet-affiliated imaging groups serving Florida. The AI assistant answers based on FAQ content covering patient policies, payments, exams, and enhanced breast cancer detection, as well as specific address and contact information for all 18 Florida imaging center locations (5 RIA + 13 Radiology Regional).

Cost, Payment & Insurance
  • Accepted payment methods and credit card fees
  • Whether payment plans are available
  • How to pay a bill online
  • Who is responsible for pre-authorization
Exam-Related Questions
  • Whether a female technologist can be requested
  • What exams are always performed by female technologists
Enhanced Breast Cancer Detection
  • Whether EBCD produces a separate report
  • Where EBCD centers are located
  • The cost to add EBCD to a mammogram
  • Whether insurance covers EBCD
General & Policies
  • Current masking policy

  • How to request a school or work note
Florida Location Questions
  • Find the nearest RIA or Radiology Regional location by city or area
  • Get the full address and phone number for a specific center
  • Ask about imaging services available at a specific location
  • Ask about office hours for a specific location
✕
What a Caller Cannot Do
  • Ask about locations outside of Florida (other states are not included in this demo)
  • Book or reschedule an appointment through the call (no scheduling system is connected)
  • Ask about specific pricing or exam cost quotes (these vary by location)
  • Make payments or discuss billing details over the call
  • Ask about Radiology Regional locations outside of Southwest Florida
  • Receive personalized medical advice or a diagnosis
  • Speak to a live human agent — this is an AI-only demo
  • Complete an agent transfer (the handoff will be attempted but not connected in the demo)

How the Call Works
1
Dial the demo number
Call the demo phone number listed above to connect to the AI assistant.
2
Greeting & introduction
The assistant welcomes the caller and briefly explains how the demo works.
3
Ask a question in natural language
No special phrasing needed — speak naturally as you would to a patient coordinator.
4
AI responds from available content
Answers are drawn from FAQ data and all 18 Florida location records.
5
Ask follow-up questions freely
The conversation is multi-turn — callers can continue asking or switch topics.
6
Out-of-scope questions
If the assistant can't answer, it will gracefully attempt to transfer to an agent. The transfer will not complete in the demo environment.
7
End the call
Say "goodbye" or simply hang up when finished.
💡 Testing Tip

Try asking about a specific city — for example, "Do you have a location near Cape Coral?" or "What's the address for the Fort Pierce office?" — to see how location lookup works across both imaging groups.
